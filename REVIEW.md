# Review Instructions and Checklist

Tips and tricks to ensure a smooth review process.
Please read this carefully and apply the changes if necessary.

EasyChair Checklist:

* All authors must be listed with full FIRST NAME and LAST NAME, incl. their email and affiliation.
* The paper must be submitted to the proper subtrack on EasyChair (Working Note papers and Quest for Insights papers are separate).
* Make sure that the title, list of authors (incl. affiliations), and the abstract of the paper, and the information provided on EasyChair are the same.
* In addition to their comments, paper reviewers need to prepare a list of questions for the authors to discuss during the Q&A part of the presentation.

Paper Checklist:

* The paper must use [the official LaTeX or Word template](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/16Ce2HNFvGy0AH4exB17i8RDRniXIWbpT).
* Papers must not be longer than 5 pages, with an extra page for references.
* The introduction must include the name that the teams used during the challenge.
* At the end of the paper, there needs to be a statement/disclosure on the use of AI.
* The submission must include the required references and cite the relevant NewsImages papers (3 papers in total, see [README](https://github.com/Informfully/Challenges)).
* The paper must include a Results Section where you compare your run performance with the baselines (this is the information sent to you after the online survey has been completed).
* There needs to be a link to the workflow for each of your runs. Ideally, each team has its own GitHub repository. If this is not the case, then please put a link to [the backup copy hosted in the Challenge repository](https://github.com/Informfully/Challenges/tree/main/newsimages26/workflows).
